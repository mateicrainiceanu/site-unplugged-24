Who are we?



Unplugged Lenau

Meet our dedicated robotics team, an embodiment of passion and commitment towards advancing STEM education. With a shared love for technology and innovation, our team tirelessly works to inspire and empower the next generation of engineers, programmers, and scientists. Through various outreach programs, workshops, and competitions, we aim to spread the joy of learning and discovery in STEM fields.




Starting in 2022, with our participation in Nokia events, we have established and maintained a very close relationship with this large corporation. They have helped us overcome the obstacles encountered along our team's journey and eventually became sponsors for us. Most importantly, we had discussions and meetings with one of our mentors: Mr. Florin Ciocan, Innovation & Ecosystem Manager at Nokia Romania. He provided us with the most support and assistance, and also gave us the opportunity to learn about adapting and applying industrial automation techniques in the field of FTC.





Our team has started off the new year wonderfully, establishing a strong and enduring relationship with the "Confident Dr. Talpoș" Dental Medicine Cabinet.We've had a meeting with the cabinet's staff, during which we introduced our team and this season's robot. We also had the opportunity to receive a tour of the cabinet, where Dr. Șerban Talpoș showed and explained the functioning of their resin-based 3D printer, the 3D intraoral scanner, and the dental microscope. Dr. Șerban Talpoș, Dr. Mălina Popa, and Dr. Roxana Ghircău Radu are among the staff members who have assisted and guided us, offering us endless possibilities both now and in the future.
